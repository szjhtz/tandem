# Tandem Compliance Starter Pack

This folder contains public starter documents for teams evaluating Tandem in regulated or security-sensitive AI workflows. The documents are intended for CISOs, compliance teams, platform engineers, and deployers who need a practical view of what Tandem supports today, what remains the deployer's responsibility, and what Tandem plans to add.

These documents are not legal advice, a conformity assessment, or a complete compliance statement for any deployment. EU AI Act obligations depend on the use case, deployment model, party role, data processed, sector-specific rules, and the deployer's governance program.

## Documents

- [AI Act control mapping](AI_ACT_CONTROL_MAPPING.md) maps selected EU AI Act articles to current Tandem controls, deployer responsibilities, and planned Tandem work.
- [Article 50 transparency](ARTICLE_50_TRANSPARENCY.md) describes intended visible labeling for AI-generated and AI-assisted Tandem content.
- [Annex IV technical documentation template](ANNEX_IV_TECHNICAL_DOCUMENTATION_TEMPLATE.md) provides a fill-in template for deployment-specific technical documentation.
- [Deployer instructions](DEPLOYER_INSTRUCTIONS.md) gives safe operating guidance for regulated workflows.
- [Limitations and responsibilities](LIMITATIONS_AND_RESPONSIBILITIES.md) separates current Tandem capabilities from deployer responsibilities and known gaps.

For the higher-level readiness brief, see [Tandem EU AI Act Readiness](../EU_AI_ACT_COMPLIANCE.md).

## Recommended Use

1. Start with the [AI Act control mapping](AI_ACT_CONTROL_MAPPING.md) to understand current and planned coverage.
2. Review [limitations and responsibilities](LIMITATIONS_AND_RESPONSIBILITIES.md) before using Tandem in a regulated workflow.
3. Adapt the [Annex IV template](ANNEX_IV_TECHNICAL_DOCUMENTATION_TEMPLATE.md) for the specific deployment.
4. Use [deployer instructions](DEPLOYER_INSTRUCTIONS.md) as the starting point for operator training and workflow runbooks.
5. Track [Article 50 transparency](ARTICLE_50_TRANSPARENCY.md) as a baseline UI and artifact-labeling requirement.
